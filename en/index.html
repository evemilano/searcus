<competitor-intelligence />

SEO Competitive Intelligence

Systematic SEO competitor analysis that maps the full competitive landscape at signal level. We dissect competitor keyword portfolios, reverse-engineer their site architecture via crawl comparison, model SERP share across priority query clusters, and audit backlink gap differentials. The output is actionable competitive intelligence for SEO: a ranked opportunity matrix that quantifies where your competitors are outranking you, why, and the exact interventions needed to close the gap — from content structure to authority acquisition.
