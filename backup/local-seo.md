## Local SEO: We Rank Your Business in Google Results

Every day, people search for services and products online. Some of these searches are very specific and related to the user's location. It becomes essential for any business to be present in geo-localized results.

The objective of a Local SEO strategy is to bring visibility to commercial activities operating in a specific geographic area. For any local business (shop, restaurant, hotel), it is fundamental to secure the best positions in search results and appear in Google Maps.

## What is Local SEO?
Local SEO includes all on-site and off-site activities that aim to increase the visibility of the website on search engines for geo-localized searches. The user who finds the commercial activity can reach the business website, get directions, or make telephone contact.

## Local Searches
Local searches are all those searches that users type to find local shops and businesses near a geographic area. Examples: dentist Geneva, restaurant Zurich, Lugano airport, accountant Bern.

In certain cases, Google deduces the user's search intent and automatically provides geolocalized results even without defining it.

## Examples of Local Searches
- Tailored suits Geneva
- Investigation agency Zurich
- Lawyer Lugano
- Hotel Bern
